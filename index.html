About Me

I'm a 2nd year ECE student with a passion for electronics, embedded systems, and IoT technologies. My journey started with basic circuit building and has evolved into hands-on experience with microcontrollers, PCB design, and IoT projects.

I have practical experience with Arduino, ESP8266/ESP32, and STM32 microcontrollers. I'm proficient in communication protocols like UART, SPI, I2C, and wireless technologies including WiFi, Bluetooth, and LoRaWAN. I enjoy designing PCBs, developing firmware, and creating end-to-end solutions.

Beyond academics, I'm passionate about robotics, contributing to college projects, and continuously learning new technologies. I believe in applying theoretical knowledge to practical problems and taking part in innovation challenges and hackathons.
